Renaissance Academy

Excellence in Academics, Character and Leadership

Pre-K

Renaissance Academy is the leading provider of primary education along with islamic studies. Our Elementary School teachers are trained with emphases on human cognitive and psychological development of your child.

Elementary

Renaissance offers a balanced High School curriculum that incorporates the Texas State graduation requirements and offers students the opportunity to go above and beyond to be more aggressive in their preparation to be successful at the college they choose to enroll in.

Middle School

Renaissance Academy has adopted the Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills (TEKS) curriculum standards and has added to them a strong program in Arabic Language, Quran and Islamic Studies.
